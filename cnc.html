Machining Projects

Machine Shop Trainings

Summary: As a part of the Machine Shop Team at Texas Inventionworks, I conduct multiple two-hour training sessions on the manual mill and the manual lathe each week. In our hands-on training sessions, we focus on the fundamentals, including machining intuition and safety. In the lathe training, we teach students how to precisely machine aluminum rings, and for the mill, we machine a dog tag.

Skills: Manual Mill and Manual Lathe Proficiency, Machining, Teaching

Bolt Action Pen (IN PROGRESS)

Summary: Currently developing a detailed Instructables guide for manufacturing a custom bolt action pen, documenting the complete fabrication process from raw material selection through final assembly. The guide combines precision machining techniques with accessible documentation to help makers at various skill levels successfully complete the project.

Problem Statement: Create an engaging Instructables guide to encourage more students to regularly visit the machine shop and interact with the professional and student staff.

Skills: Machining, CAD, Manufacturing
